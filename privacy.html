Privacy Policy for Timer App

Last updated: February 2026

Timer App ("the App") is developed and maintained by José Salinas. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Timer App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No personal data is sent to external servers.
  • No location, contact, or device data is collected.

The App functions entirely offline and does not communicate with a backend server.

2. Local Data Storage

Timer settings such as round counts, durations, and sound preferences may be stored locally on your device to enable the app’s functionality.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

3. Permissions

The App may request device permissions strictly required for functionality, such as audio playback or notifications for active timers.

No sensitive permissions (such as contacts, camera, microphone, or location) are accessed.

4. Third-Party Services

Timer App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since no personal information is collected at all, there is no risk of child data processing.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
